Genesis Energy, L.P. to Present at the Wells Fargo Securities 2015 Energy Symposium
Genesis Energy, L.P. (GEL) announced today that Grant Sims, CEO, will make a presentation at the Wells Fargo 2015 Energy Symposium on December 8 - 9, 2015, in New York City. Mr. Sims will discuss Genesis Energy’s performance, growth strategies and financial objectives.

Genesis Energy’s presentation will take place on Tuesday, December 8. The presentation is not being webcast but the presentation will be made available on the Partnership’s website at www.genesisenergy.com under “Investors – Presentations”.

Genesis Energy, L.P. is a diversified midstream energy master limited partnership headquartered in Houston, Texas. Genesis’ operations include onshore and offshore pipeline transportation, refinery services, marine transportation and supply and logistics. Genesis’ operations are primarily located in Texas, Louisiana, Arkansas, Mississippi, Alabama, Florida, Wyoming and the Gulf of Mexico.
